The Idea

The webpage will work using the coordination of 3 elements,the user who is facing the issue ,the people who will help to rectify and the moderators who will maintain the decorum of the website. The main motto of the webpage will be to provide accessible judgement free friendly solutions which will be totally confidential. The webpage will focus on the specific problems listed by the user and provide the best case solution by helping the user to chat/talk freely on the online self help webpage .

Website Specifications

  • The verification will be done by collecting information such as name,gender,age(22+ will be the basic requirement) etc.
  • The user would reqire to submit a certificate indicating completion of basic psychology course.
  • These volunteers will also be required to clear case studies to be selected as a member of the webpage.
  • Soothing Songs,Motivational,eye opening and comedy movies, documentaries and books will be uploaded which will enable the user to get a little distracted from the personal issues and think positively.
  • The moderators for the time being will be humans (till the time AI advances to a an extent where it can understand human emotions).
